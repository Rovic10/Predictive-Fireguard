ABOUT PREDICTIVE FIREGUARD

To predict fire outbreaks in barangay Pinagbuhatan, Bambang, and San Nicolas, Predictive Fireguard offers several essential services, including providing early warnings and alerts, assessing fire risks in different areas, suggesting preventive measures, analyzing historical data for trend identification, engaging the local community with fire safety information, and continuously monitoring environmental conditions. These services collectively aid in reducing the risk of fire incidents and ensuring the safety of the Barangays residents while promoting efficient resource allocation and community engagement.
